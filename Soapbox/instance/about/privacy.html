Personal Data We Collect and Process

By default we do not process any personal data.

Technically necessary data (IP address and user agent), are automatically processed by our server(s) when you visit any of our services. Our server(s) uses your IP address to deliver content to your browser. Under Article 6 (1) b GDPR we have a legitimate legal basis for processing this data.

Logs

Our server(s) generates logs automatically, these logs (depending on the service see additional policies / exceptions) may contain data such as your IP address, user agent and URL you accessed from. However these logs are not retained and are kept for no more then 24 hours. By default our server(s) don't save any logs, the only time we save logs is for fixing problems or investigating attacks, only for a period of 24 hours.

Registration Data

Some services on silkky.cloud offer the option to register an account. During the registration process you may be required to provide personal information such as a username or email address to allocate your data. Our legal basis for collecting this data is Article 6 (1) b GDPR. You can delete your account(s) by accessing the respective service or contact us.

Storage and Access to Data

Where possible your data is encrypted. All of your data is stored on our server(s) in a secure data center owned and operated by Hetzner Online GmbH in Helsinki, Finland. We will never look at or share any personal data with a third party.

Deleting Account(s)

Deleting an account will destroy all data associated with your account, you can delete your account at any time. Please note that usernames used with deleted accounts might still unavailable to use after deletion.

Federation

Federation enables users on different servers to communicate with each other such as chatting or sharing files. Some of our services offer federation, the nature of federation allows data to shared across different servers or entities. Sharing this data with other services / providers is entirely the user's decision.
